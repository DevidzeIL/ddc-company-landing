# DDC Company Design System

## Typography
- Font: "JetBrains Mono", monospace for all text
- Headings: bold, uppercase
- Body: regular weight

## Colors
- Background: #1a1a1a with dark fabric/carbon texture overlay
- Text primary: #ffffff
- Text secondary: #999999
- Accent purple: #7B61FF (marquee bands)
- CTA border: #FF4444 (red outline buttons)
- Stars: #FFD700 gold
- Submit button: #2196F3 blue
- Cards bg: white with dark text

## Layout
- Max-width: 1200px centered
- Full-bleed marquee bands
- Dark theme throughout

## Components
- Marquee ticker (horizontal infinite scroll)
- Case cards with Было/Стало
- Stacked/tilted service cards
- Vertical timeline for steps
- Contact form
